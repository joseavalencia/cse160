I was inspired by the creeper from Minecraft. I wanted to make a blocky animal that looks like a creeper. The creeper has 3 joints—head, legs, and feet. I also made a Cone.js file similar to Cube.js to put a party cone on its head as the non‐cube primitive requirement. Use the Poke Mode button or Shift-click the canvas to enter party mode!
