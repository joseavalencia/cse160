Welcome to the Maze!

You’re about to play hide-and-seek with a wandering Creeper.
Click “Call Creeper” to spawn him somewhere deep in the maze. Then use your keys to track him down.

How to Play

  1. Click Call Creeper to send him to a random spot.
  2. Use W/A/S/D to walk around the grid.
  3. Use the mouse (click & drag) to look around.
  4. When you think you found him, press Animate Creeper to watch him walk.

Key-Bindings

  • W: Move Forward
  • S: Move Backward
  • A: Strafe Left
  • D: Strafe Right
  • Q / E: Pan camera left / right
  • ↑ / ↓: Move camera Up / Down

Build & Break

  • Z (press): Place a wood-plank block in front of you
  • X (press): Break the block in front of you
